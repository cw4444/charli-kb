# Creative Recovery And Input Fasting

The useful claim in the creativity material is that creative output often fails because the mind is overfilled, not under-informed. Constant input leaves little room for digestion, pattern recognition, boredom, or associative thought.

The proposed repair is an input fast:

- Reduce the default stream of podcasts, feeds, news, and social scrolling.
- Spend time in silence, walking, slow reading, and unstructured thought.
- Let unfinished thoughts surface before trying to produce.
- Separate generative thinking from evaluative editing.
- Create privately before sharing or optimizing.

This fits the broader wiki pattern well. Notion can remain the capture dump, but `charli-kb` should be the digested layer. The point is not to hoard more inputs; it is to create enough space that the useful inputs become usable.
