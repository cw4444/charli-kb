# Peter Steinberger

Peter Steinberger is a software developer and writer whose articles in this wiki are used as practical source material on agentic engineering workflows.

The retained ideas are about agent-readable repos, direct communication with agents, small blast radius, CLI feedback loops, and development workflows where human judgment becomes the scarce resource.
