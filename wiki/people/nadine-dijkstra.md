# Nadine Dijkstra

Nadine Dijkstra is a cognitive neuroscientist associated with work on visual imagery, perception, and reality monitoring. In `charli-kb`, she is currently relevant for the reality-threshold model: the idea that perceived and imagined signals may be judged real when their subjective strength crosses a threshold.
